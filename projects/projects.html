Learning-based 3D Computer Vision (2023)

This project is a series of course assignments for 16-825 Learning for 3D Vision at Carnegie Mellon University. The course covers 3D computer vision topics ranging from camera models and rendering, to deep learning-based 3D reconstruction, classification, and segmentation. The projects are primarily implemented using PyTorch3D.
